# Intelligent-Mission-Planning-Analytics-Platform-for-Drone-Operations

## Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Business Analytics Dashboard using SQL, Excel, and Power BI to transform raw business data into actionable insights. The objective was to analyze business performance, identify trends, track key performance indicators (KPIs), and support data-driven decision-making through effective visualization and reporting.

---

## Dataset Selection

The dataset was selected to represent real-world business operations containing information related to sales transactions, revenue, profit, products, customers, regions, and order details.

The dataset was chosen based on the following criteria:

* Availability of multiple business dimensions for analysis.
* Presence of numerical and categorical attributes.
* Ability to generate meaningful KPIs.
* Suitability for SQL querying and Power BI visualization.
* Realistic business use cases involving sales and performance monitoring.

Before analysis, the dataset was reviewed for completeness, consistency, and analytical relevance.

---

## Data Preparation and Cleaning

Raw business data often contains inconsistencies and missing values. Several preprocessing steps were performed to ensure data quality:

* Removal of duplicate records.
* Handling missing or null values.
* Standardization of column formats.
* Validation of sales and profit figures.
* Correction of inconsistent categorical values.
* Data type conversions where necessary.

These steps ensured that the data was reliable for reporting and analysis.

---

## SQL Analysis

SQL was used to perform exploratory analysis and business reporting.

Key SQL operations included:

* Data filtering and sorting.
* Aggregate calculations using SUM(), AVG(), COUNT(), and MAX().
* GROUP BY analysis for business segmentation.
* Revenue and profit calculations.
* Region-wise and product-wise performance analysis.
* Trend analysis using date-based queries.
* KPI generation through custom SQL queries.

Sample business questions addressed:

* Which products generated the highest revenue?
* Which regions contributed the most profit?
* What are the monthly sales trends?
* Which customer segments perform best?
* What are the top-performing categories?

SQL helped transform raw transactional data into meaningful business metrics.

---

## Power BI Dashboard Development

After analysis, Power BI was used to create an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.

The dashboard includes:

### KPI Cards

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin

### Visualizations

* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Profit Trend Analysis
* Product Performance
* Regional Sales Distribution
* Category-Level Analysis
* Customer Segment Analysis

### Interactive Features

* Dynamic Filters
* Slicers
* Drill-Down Reports
* Cross-Filtering
* Interactive Data Exploration

Power Query was used for data transformation and modeling before dashboard development.

---

## Key Insights Generated

The dashboard enables users to:

* Monitor overall business performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Analyze regional trends.
* Track revenue growth.
* Evaluate profitability across business segments.
* Support strategic decision-making through visual analytics.
